About the Project

[cite_start]Sunnyside Nodes is a virtual town where each institution—a hospital, police department, school, or skate park—hosts a single artistic work[cite: 20]. [cite_start]This space serves as both an architectural metaphor and a unique venue to engage with different artistic perspectives[cite: 21].

[cite_start]Our project is rooted in the idea of the "archaic smile"—the serene, enigmatic expression found in ancient Greek sculpture[cite: 3]. [cite_start]This smile is not just a facial expression; it embodies a universal aspiration for joy and a deep human connection to beauty and harmony[cite: 4]. [cite_start]We see the archaic smile as the reverse of the "uncanny valley"[cite: 9]. [cite_start]It avoids discomfort by embracing its own artificiality, aiming for human resonance rather than a perfect copy of reality[cite: 10, 11].

[cite_start]In the same way, we explore how Artificial Intelligence in art can create synthetic gestures that, while artificial, resonate deeply with an audience[cite: 14]. [cite_start]Sunnyside Nodes explores AI's dual role as both an artistic collaborator and a servant to human needs, adding layers of meaning to its "synthetic smiles"[cite: 17].

[cite_start]Ultimately, Sunnyside Nodes is a vision of atemporal beauty, joy, and empathy[cite: 18]. [cite_start]It is a space where the positive side of humanity is amplified, accelerated, and saturated[cite: 19].
